UniFirst Corporation Environmental Compliance Experience

Streamlined regulatory tracking for 260+ locations, developed detailed analytical reports, and collaborated on wastewater quality improvements to enhance sustainability efforts.

Description of My Impact

During my co-op at UniFirst Corporation, I worked to enhance environmental compliance across all 260 locations by developing and maintaining a centralized tracker that streamlined data access for the entire environmental team. I reviewed daily maintenance logs, dissolved air flotation records, and wastewater lab reports, compiling monthly, quarterly, and semi-annual summaries while also supporting permit applications and surveys. My role included hands-on plant visits, where I recalibrated pH meters, toured wastewater facilities, and collaborated with plant managers to identify opportunities for process improvements. I also prepared and presented detailed reports, charts, and permit analyses in meetings aimed at optimizing wastewater quality, combining technical precision with actionable insights.

As the only co-op within a four person team, it was critical to remain easily adaptable and complete tasks as quickly and efficiently as possible. During my time in this position, I gained very valuable skills in organization and synchronously juggling a large amount of projects with little oversight or isntruction from my suprevisor.

Overview of the Company

UniFirst Corporation offers a range of services, including uniform rental, laundering, mending, embroidery, pickup and delivery, and facility services such as restroom and cleaning products. With a workforce of more than 14,000 employees, UniFirst serves over 300,000 customers, outfitting over 2 million workers across various industries. The company has implemented comprehensive environmental compliance programs to promote responsible business practices and minimize its environmental impact such as wastewater managemnet, water treatment awards, and ESG initiatives.
